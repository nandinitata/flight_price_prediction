SkySaver: US Flight Price Predictor

Exploring and Analyzing US Domestic Flight Data Patterns

This project analyzes flight data across different US cities, investigating patterns in fares, routes, and passenger behavior.

Introduction

Welcome to SkySaver, a comprehensive flight price prediction system for US domestic flights. This platform utilizes advanced machine learning techniques to analyze historical flight data and predict future price trends. Our analysis covers major routes between US cities, helping travelers make informed decisions about their flight bookings.
